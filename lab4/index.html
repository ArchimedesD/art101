Lab 4 - Pseudocode

Challenge

This lab involved brainstorming a list of tasks and breaking them down into sub-tasks. We then rewrote these steps in the form of pseudocode and implemented them into the comments of a javascript file. This challenge was to have us think in terms of pseudocode when coding.

Problems

The problem I faced was trying to think in terms of psuedocode. It felt unusual to talk about everyday tasks or games and break them down in coding terms.

Results

Our result is a list of pseudocode detailing the implementation of Snake Game.
